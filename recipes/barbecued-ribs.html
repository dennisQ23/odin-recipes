Classic Barbecued Ribs

These are some classic ribs that your mother used to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) package pork spareribs
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons celery salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Sprinkle ribs with garlic salt and place into a roasting pan.

  3. Stir ketchup, vinegar, sugar, celery salt, chili powder, salt, dry mustard, paprika, and black pepper together in a bowl; pour over the ribs.

  4. Cook, basting with the pan drippings every half-hour, until the meat pulls away easily from the bone, about 90 minutes.
